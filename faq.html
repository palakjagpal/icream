FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are your operating hours?

Our typical operating hours are Monday to Sunday, from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Please check our "Contact Us" page for any special holiday hours or temporary changes.

Do you offer dairy-free or vegan options?

Yes, we are proud to offer a variety of delicious dairy-free and vegan ice cream options made with almond milk, coconut milk, or oat milk bases. Look for the special labels in our product section!

Can I place an order for delivery?

Absolutely! We partner with several local delivery services. You can find us on [Delivery App 1], [Delivery App 2], and [Delivery App 3]. Visit their apps or websites to place your order.

Do you cater for events or parties?

Yes, we do! iCream offers catering services for events of all sizes, from small gatherings to large parties. Please contact us directly via email at events@icream.com or call us at (123) 456-7890 to discuss your needs and get a custom quote.

Are your products gluten-free?

We offer several gluten-free ice cream flavors and toppings. While we take precautions to prevent cross-contamination, our facility does handle gluten-containing products. Please inform our staff about any severe allergies.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and cash. We also support mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

How can I apply for a job at iCream?

We're always looking for passionate and enthusiastic individuals to join our team! Please send your resume and a cover letter to jobs@icream.com. We'll get in touch if there's a suitable opening.
